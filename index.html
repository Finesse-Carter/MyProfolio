Finesse J. Carter Boston, Massachusetts native and resident who is passionate about making managing businesses easier for others. In 2016, I launched an online clothing line that has propelled me into the software engineer that I am today. I wasn’t always in IT, I studied electrical at Madison Park High and attempted college. Finding college to be a challenge, I transitioned into the workforce, just as my parents did (Union Carpenters) as a Union Laborer for 6 years working on commercial projects throughout my career. Years went by then I realized I needed to take full control of my life and do things I love. I came across Resilient Coders and developed skills in HTML, CSS & Javascript building full stack web Applications.

I create designs that adapt to the capabilities of many devices and screen sizes. I create alliterates and use predominantly HTML and CSS so I won’t waste time mocking up every template as a static visual. I use visuals to indicate a creative direction (color, texture and typography). You’ll have plenty of opportunities to review our work and provide feedback. I’ll either share a Dropbox, Google Drive folder or Github repository or development site with you and we’ll have regular, possibly daily contact by either phone, email, or Slack. If at any stage you change your mind about what you want delivered or aren’t happy with the direction our work is taking, you’ll pay in full for the time we’ve spent working until that point and may terminate the contract.
